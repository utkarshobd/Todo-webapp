About Todo App

Welcome to Your Personal Todo Manager

Simplify your life by organizing tasks efficiently!

App Features

  • Easy Task Management

    Add, update, and delete todos with just a few clicks.

  • Persistent Storage

    Your todos are saved and remain accessible across sessions.

  • Clean and Intuitive Interface

    User-friendly design makes task tracking a breeze.

How to Use the App

  1. Add a Todo
    Click on the "Add Todo" section, fill in the title and description.
  2. View Todos
    All your todos are displayed in the table below the add form.
  3. Update a Todo
    Click the "Update" button next to any todo to modify its details.
  4. Delete a Todo
    Use the "Delete" button to remove a todo you no longer need.
Quick Tips
  • Keep todos concise and actionable
  • Update todos as you complete them
  • Don't hesitate to delete completed todos
